FAQ
Every Question. Answered.

How accurate is the data?

Our goal is usable data you can put into your pipeline with confidence. Match rates vary by market and niche, but we continuously monitor and refine quality. We'll walk through what to expect for your vertical on your demo call.

How often do I receive new data?

All plans receive daily data refreshes. Your audiences are updated every day with fresh high-intent signals so you're always working the most current prospects in your market.

Do you integrate with my CRM?

Yes. At minimum you can export CSV files compatible with major CRMs and dialers. Growth and Custom plans include help setting up automated workflows that push InMarket.ai data directly into your tools.

What about compliance and privacy?

We take compliance seriously and operate within relevant data and privacy regulations. We cover our approach and your responsibilities for contacting prospects on every demo call.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. We want you to stay because the data is driving results — not because you're locked in. Most customers start small, prove ROI quickly, then scale aggressively.

What do I need to get started?

Someone who can follow up with leads and a basic CRM or spreadsheet to track conversations. If you already have a dialer or outbound motion, we simply make it far more effective with better data.
